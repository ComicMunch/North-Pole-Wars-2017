# North-Pole-Wars-2017
North Pole Wars a short story that cartoonist, Comic Munch conceived 5 days before Christmas back in 2027 which he released as a short serial on his Instagram page. Here is a revised version with added story elements.

Welcome to the **Northern Snows**, where the magic of the season meets the might of a **Living Soul**. This project showcases the 2017 original art and story of **Peppermint of Cinnamon Alley** and her war against the **Abominable Snow Beasts**.
