# 🎄 North Pole Wars: 2017 Chronicles

Welcome to the **Northern Snows**, where the magic of the season meets the might of a **Living Soul**. This project showcases the 2017 original art and story of **Peppermint of Cinnamon Alley** and her war against the **Abominable Snow Beasts**.

---

## 📖 The Epic Saga

### I. The Call to Arms
Peppermint and her family at **Pine Farms** were just trying to make the most holly-jolly trees in the multiverse. But when the **Abominable Snow Beasts** began wreaking havoc, Pepper didn't grab coal—she grabbed her **Maple Axe**.
